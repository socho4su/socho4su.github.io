Our Portfolio of Experiences

With five years of combined experience in the Student Union, without a doubt, no one can compare to our level of expertise. We've worked in every level of Student Government.

Our work on all levels

Serving as Caucus & SU Presidents, we've worked with ambitous students to develop comprehesive projects and wonderful events.
Serving on the School Leadership Team (SLT), we've been able to work closely with the administration and the parents association to advance school policy changes. Everything from Homework Policies and Freshmen Out For Frees goes through their legislative committee.
Serving on the Manhattan Borough Student Advisory Council, we've worked with other school leaders, as well as DOE officials to develop proposals and plans for the DOE. These include College Readiness Programs and plans to benefit not only us, but the entire borough as well.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra-curricular activities, and other areas of school life. We will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away from the Student Union.
