So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra- curricular activities, and other areas of school life. It will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away.
