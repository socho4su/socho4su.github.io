Finance & Budget

Having run events, allocated funds, and made hard financial decisions, Matthew & Tahseen know best how essential it is that the Student Union's Budget is stable & secure for years to come. We present a new financial model with a steady but progressive growth.

Structural Solutions

A strong Student Union is strengthened by students who are well aware of what is going on in the SU and are not apathetic to developments within their community. A strong Student Union is made up of extensive and varied representation among all demographics of Stuyvesant.

Inter-School Coalition

The DOE makes a good amount of decisions regarding student life. It's not open to the students that are affected most by this. Our Coalition will fight to provide students with lobbying power within the DOE. It's long term, but it's a plan.

Shooting for the Stars

Previous Student Unions have begun long-term large scale initiatives. We plan to continue many of them. We also have our own plans. These initiatives may take up to a decade to accomplish, but will be better for Stuyvesant as a whole.

  • Our Long-Term Initiatives

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra-curricular activities, and other areas of school life. We will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away from the Student Union.
