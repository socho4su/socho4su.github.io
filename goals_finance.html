Finance & Budget

Having run events, allocated funds, and made hard financial decisions, Matthew & Tahseen know best how essential it is that the Student Union's Budget is stable & secure for years to come. We present a new model with steady but progressive growth.

The Current Model

How It Works

The model the Student Union has been using recently is to simply fund clubs & pubs at the start of the year. Then Caucus officials work throughout the year to plan events and bring in revenue for the SU to sustain itself. Although this structure may work, it's not very reliable & leaves the SU with too much of a focus on finances throughout the year. There are safer, stronger, & more stable alternatives.

Expenses

We spend money on events such as SING! & our dances. However, our largest expenditure is funding the Clubs & Pubs of Stuyvesant.

Revenue

Our revenue comes from events that we plan & sponsorships that we recieve.

Our Scrapping Model

What's Different?

Our model decreases the risk of any loss to the Student Union account. It also sets up a plan that will guarantee growth if followed by future Student Unions.

Base Funding

The Base budget will be allocated to SING! & Caucus Events. The Base will not be touched by any other organization. This will provide the revenue for years to come.

Revenue Funding

Our revenue comes from the investments we make with the Base amount. This money will be allocated largely to Clubs & Pubs. About 25% will be returned to Base in order to increase our revenue for future years.

Stability & Security

Our plan allows us to safely secure the Base funding for future years. This Base will return about 30% of revenue every year. This revenue will be used to fund activities that do not bring in income, such as Club/Pub funding.

Improving Returns & Protecting Assets

Caucus Accountability

Every Caucus will now be required to submit Cost/Revenue analsysis to the SU before having a budget approved for it's event. The Sophomore & Freshman Caucus did it this year and didn't lose a dime.

  • Facebook

Club Pub Accountability

Clubs & Pubs have the most funding from the SU, yet they have the least accountability. We will audit clubs to make sure they spend their budget as allocated. We will also collect assets at the end of the year to avoid losing SU funded assests.

  • Facebook

Sponsorships

The Student Union Budget Department will focus on getting more sponsorships throughout the year for its events. Sponsors such as Khan's Tutorial, Kweller Prep, & Dos Toros have been very helping in funding events, as well as Clubs & Pubs.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra- curricular activities, and other areas of school life. It will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away.
