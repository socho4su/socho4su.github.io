Shooting For The Stars

Previous Student Unions have begun long-term large scale initiatives. We plan to continue many of them. We also have our own plans. These initiatives may take up to a decade to accomplish, but will be better for Stuyvesant as a whole.

Our Long Term Plans

5th Floor Balcony

The Battery Park City Authority, the Department of Education, & the School Construction Authority are large bureaucratic organizations that take time to collaborate and work together. We've been bearing the painstakingly long process for a year and there isn't an end in sight. Matthew & Tahseen pledge to continue to work to open the 5th floor balcony no matter how long it takes.

  • Facebook

Locker Trading System

With the switch from Daedalus to eSchoolData, we've completely lost track of how locker assignments would normally work. However, the SU does now have the resources to run a locker trading system. This means, as soon as the school year begins and lockers are assigned, Matthew & Tahseen will jump in to implement a locker trading system.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra-curricular activities, and other areas of school life. We will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away from the Student Union.
