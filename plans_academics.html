Academics

We believe the Student Union has a responsibility to make sure that the students of Stuyvesant High School have all the resources they need to succeed academically.

Our Three Step Plan

Enforcing the Homework Policy

Advertising ARISTA & AIS Tutoring & Support Programs

Collaborative Study Guides

Enforcing The Homework Policy

We will work to ensure that homework is being assigned according to Stuyvesantâ€™s Homework Policy. If homework is being assigned improperly, we will take the steps needed to ensure that the policy is enforced.

We're also working with the Parents Association & the School Leadership Team to develop a new homework policy, one that would greatly benefit the student body in terms of how much time each student spends per night on homework..

Advertising ARISTA & AIS Tutoring & Support Programs

The SU has a section on its website dedicated to student resources. These resources have textbooks and other essential tools to help students, but that's not enough. We will build an ARISTA website and fill it with all our valuable resources. In addition, we will streamline the website so it serves as a hub for all tutoring & support programs throughout Stuyvesant, whether or not they relate to ARISTA. It will work to provide alternatives to peer tutoring, which is known for its large backlog.

Collaborative Study Guides

The SU will create an Academic Department that will work with ARISTA to create collaborative study guides before finals week. This will ensure that the guides are comprehensive and well thought-out. The collaboration will allow the writers to bounce ideas that different teachers provide. It will serve a one stop jumpstart guide for studying for final exams. We will start with a few finals and then expand based on the popularity and feasibility of the guides.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra-curricular activities, and other areas of school life. We will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away from the Student Union.
