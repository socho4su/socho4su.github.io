Daily Life

The School Leadership Team (SLT) set a goal this year to do everything in its power to help students in their day to day life. The Student Union can easily provide much needed support to continue to help them in their endeavors. In addition, communicating information like Gym Schedules can be very helpful & with new Student Union Task Forces, anyone can work with SU resources to advance their goal.

Student Union Task Forces

  • Facebook

There are many students out there that are concerned about problems relating to day-to-day life within Stuy and want to fix it. They know they have the ability to do so, they just don’t have the resources to do it.

  • Facebook

The Student Union will have ongoing suggestion & task force sign-ups where students that have a goal can sign up and work with the Student Union. This way experienced and motivated students have access to the Student Unions networks and assets.

  • Facebook

When working with us, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away. This will only work to improve our efficency when it comes time to work on projects.

Maintaining Bathrooms & Clocks

The Student Union SLT Alternate Kevin Boodram, SU SLT Rep Asher Lasday, & Sophomore Caucus Director of Daily Well-being Mansour Elsharawy worked together to make sure bathrooms were improved, locks were installed, new paper towel dispensers were added, and broken clocks were replaced. No matter what, we will make sure that someone, whether or not it be us, takes on this initiative next year.

Emailing Weekly Schedules

Sophomore Caucus Director of Public Relations Henry Zheng took on the initiative to email & share the weekly Phys Ed & Homeroom Schedules to as many sophomores as possible. This is a great program we’ve received a lot of compliments for so we we’re going to expand this into the entire school next year.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra- curricular activities, and other areas of school life. It will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away.
