Events

Events promote spirit, unity, and an environment for students to destress. They also provide the Student Union with funds to continue funding the Clubs and Pubs of Stuyvesant. Tens of thousands of dollars of Club Pub funding comes from successful event planning.

Events We'll Promote, Fund, & Support

Movie Nights, Game Nights, & Casual Events

Casual events are cheap, easy to plan, and fun to have. We’ve planned these events for long enough to know all the ins and outs. We will plan events like this every two months. If a caucus decides to take on this initiative, we will provide full support.

Seasonal Events

Events like Stuylloween are very fun & exciting. They bring people together based on a universally celebrated holiday. This brings spirit levels to an all time high. We can promise to continue seasonal events that will be fun and exciting.

Dances

Spring Fling, SophFrosh Semi-Formal, & JProm are large & fun events that are quite exciting. We will put our support behind caucuses that plan these events. We’re going to teach them how to expand & make these events more exciting and financially stable.

Ensuring Financial Accountability for Caucuses & Event Planning

This year the Sophomore & Freshman Caucuses did a comprehensive Cost/Revenue Analysis before every single event. The idea behind it was that it would allow us to make sure we were taking the lowest risk possible with the highest potential return. Without a doubt, our analysis was a success. The Sophomore & Freshman Caucuses did not lose a single dollar on any event throughout the year. Instead, they’ve successfully raised thousands of dollars in revenue for the Student Union. The same cannot be said for caucuses that did not do this.

Thus, we will require all Student Union funded activities have a formal Cost & Revenue Analysis approved by the Student Union President & Vice President before expenses are made. This will make sure that caucuses are held accountable for their expenses. In addition, it will decrease the risks we take.

So & Chowdhury for SU

So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra-curricular activities, and other areas of school life. We will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away from the Student Union.
